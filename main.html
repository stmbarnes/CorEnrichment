Patricia and Lia partnered to launch c.o.r enrichment after they met in 1995. Since that time, their work has been devoted to children with special needs. While both hold a Master's of Science degree in Occupational Therapy, in addition, Patricia holds post-graduate degree in Sensory Integration from Temple and is a certified Sensory Integration Praxis Test provider. Lia has served many years on the board of a non-profit charity serving special need children.

Lia and Patricia have extensive experience with developing and marketing therapeutic programs, caregiver education, staff training, service delivery, the Alert Program, Wilbarger Deep Pressure Touch Protocol, Therapeutic Listening Programs, Dr. Kay Toomey's SOS feeding approach, Handwriting Without Tears, DIR/Floortime model, Grey & Winner's Social Thinking, Assistive Technology, Quality of Movement, Neuro Development Techniques (NDT), Evaluations for accessibility, Ergonomics, and Universal Design.
